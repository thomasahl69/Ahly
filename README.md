# Ahly's Maze 🐶🦴

A first-person maze adventure starring **Ahly**, a little lost pug on a great big bone hunt.
Help Ahly sniff out bones, dodge the bigger dogs, and find the way through **10 different mazes**.

The whole game is a **single file** — `ahlys-maze.html`. Everything (pictures and sounds) is
baked right in, so it works **offline**. Just double-click the file and it opens in any modern
web browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox). No installing, no internet needed.

---

## How to play

**On a computer (keyboard + mouse):**
- **↑** move forward, **↓** move back
- **←** step left, **→** step right  *(the **W A S D** keys also work)*
- **Move the mouse** to look around — click once to "lock" the mouse in, or click-and-drag
- **Space bar** to jump
- **M** toggles the map · **Esc** pauses

**On a phone or tablet (touch):**
- Use the round pads on the bottom-left to move (▲ forward, ▼ back, ◀ ▶ step sideways)
- Swipe the screen, or use the **⟲ ⟳** pads, to look around
- Tap **How to Play** on the menu any time for a reminder

There's a **How to Play** button right on the start screen too.

## Home-screen settings

On the start screen there are three toggles:

- **🎵 Music** — turns the background song on or off (it plays softly during the game).
- **🔊 Sound effects** — turns the barks and yips on or off.
- **📱 Mobile controls** — shows the on-screen move/look pads. This turns on automatically
  on phones and tablets, but you can flip it on for any touch screen (or off if you'd rather
  use a keyboard).

## The goal

Each maze has a **bone bar** at the top. Collect bones to fill it up — when it's full,
Ahly moves on to the next maze. The mazes get a little bigger as you go, and Ahly gets
a little quicker. Clear all 10 to win!

## The bones

| Bone | What it does |
|------|--------------|
| 🦴 White | +1 point |
| 🎄 Christmas | +3 points |
| ✨ Gold | +5 points **and** lets Ahly walk through walls for a few seconds |
| 🔥 Fire | −5 points — stay away from these! |
| 🎁 Gold Christmas | Magic bone — zooms Ahly straight to the next maze |

## The other dogs

- **Big pugs** chase Ahly. If one catches up, Ahly simply pops back to the middle of the
  maze — nothing scary, just a fresh start. (They're slow and easy to outrun, just right for
  little players.)
- **Little pups** scurry around eating loose bones, so grab the good ones first!
- **Noah**, a friendly doodle, drops by now and then with a treat. Run over to him for a bonus.
